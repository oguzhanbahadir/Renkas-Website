Working at Canatal

A Workplace that Exceeds Your Expectations

On top of being part of something big, here’s a glimpse of the many advantages of being a member of the Canatal family:

  1. Safe and stimulating work environment
  2. Great place to evolve in
  3. Competitive salary and conditions
  4. Year-round work and overtime often available
  5. Floating days off
  6. Retirement plan and group insurance plan
  7. Structured training program
  8. Employee assistance program
  9. Great community
  10. Training program
  11. Culture of continuous improvement

Stability, security and quality of life are at the core of what we build every day.
